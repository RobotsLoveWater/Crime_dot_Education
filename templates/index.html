What you can do

Filter the data

Narrow the cases by offense, sentence length, county, and more. Every filter becomes a plain-language chip you can peel back.

Compare groups

Build cross-tabulations (crosstabs) to see how sentences differ across groups, with a heatmap and CSV export.

Read the statistics

See counts, means, medians, and distributions for any column, charted beside the numbers that back them.

Follow guided lessons

Step-by-step activities run beside a live, read-only view of the data they describe — answers are graded from the data itself.
